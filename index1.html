Cold Coffee

Cold Coffee (Cold Coffee Shake, Cold Coffee Milkshake) is a creamy cold beverage with mainly five ingredients instant coffee, cold milk, milk powder, vanilla ice cream, and sugar.

Ice Cream Cake

This festive copycat Dairy Queen ice cream cake is layered with rich chocolate ice cream, creamy chocolate fudge, crunchy Oreo pieces, and creamy vanilla ice cream, then frosted with whipped cream and finished with rainbow confetti sprinkles.

Kit Kat Ice Cream

The inside of the Kit Kat Ice Cream Bar is a blend of chocolate and wafer flavoured “frozen delight.” The outside shell consists of milk chocolate and crushed pieces of Kit Kat.
